## Overview

Zanode bills in South African Rands (ZAR). No exchange rate surprises. No hidden fees.

<Note>
  **We're currently in beta!** Billing is coming soon—enjoy free access while we build out the platform.
</Note>

## Pricing tiers

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | R0/month | Getting started, side projects |
| **Pro** | R299/month | Production apps, growing projects |
| **Team** | R799/month | Teams, agencies, high-traffic apps |

<Tip>
  All plans include automatic deploys, SSL, and SA-hosted infrastructure.
</Tip>

## What's included

### Free (R0/month)
- 3 projects
- 1 database
- 10 GB bandwidth
- Community support

### Pro (R299/month)
- 10 projects
- 5 databases
- 100 GB bandwidth
- Priority email support
- Custom domains

### Team (R799/month)
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited databases
- 500 GB bandwidth
- Priority support
- Team collaboration

## Bandwidth

Outbound data transfer is included in your plan:

| Plan | Included |
|------|----------|

<Info>
  Most apps use far less than their included bandwidth. Overage pricing will be announced when billing launches.
